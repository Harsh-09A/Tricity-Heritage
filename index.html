overview

EMBRACE NATURE, LIVE YOUR DREAM LIFESTYLE

Tricity Heritage is a premium residential and commercial project located in Sector 21, New Panvel, Navi Mumbai. It offers thoughtfully designed 1 & 2 BHK apartments alongside commercial shops, catering to diverse needs. The project enjoys a strategic location near Panvel Railway Station, ensuring seamless connectivity to Mumbai, Pune, and other key urban hubs. With its modern infrastructure and excellent access, it is well-suited for both families and professionals.

The project boasts a range of facilities and amenities aimed at elevating the living experience of its residents. From well-planned interiors to lifestyle-enhancing features, Tricity Heritage provides a perfect blend of convenience and comfort. Its prime location, coupled with quality construction, makes it an attractive choice for individuals seeking accessibility, modern living, and investment opportunities in a rapidly growing area.
